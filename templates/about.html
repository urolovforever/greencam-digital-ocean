Green Campus Initiative: Integrating Sustainability into Higher Education in Uzbekistan (GreenCamUz) is an international project developed by partner higher education institutions from Uzbekistan, Europe, and Türkiye. The project is funded by a €359,000 European Union grant under the Erasmus+ Capacity Building in Higher Education (CBHE) Strand 1 framework.

GreenCamUz is the only project in Uzbekistan funded under the CBHE Strand 1 category. It is implemented in line with the United Nations Sustainable Development Goals (SDGs) and the European Green Deal priorities.

The project aims to integrate sustainability approaches into higher education institutions. Its activities focus on curriculum modernization, capacity building for academic and administrative staff, and the implementation of green pilot initiatives across partner campuses.

Project duration: 1 October 2025 – 30 September 2028

Partner institutions:

  • 🇺🇿 Uzbekistan: Kokand University, Kokand University Andijan Branch, Tashkent International University, Republican Higher School of Business and Entrepreneurship, Central Asian Green University, Andijan State Technical Institute
  • 🇪🇺 Europe: University of Opole (Poland), Vilnius Tech University (Lithuania)
  • 🇹🇷 Türkiye: Karabuk University
