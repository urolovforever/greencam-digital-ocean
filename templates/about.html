Loyiha maqsadi:
Loyiha kelajakka yo'naltirilgan strategiyalar yordamida barqarorlikni turli ta'lim dasturlariga integratsiya qilishni maqsad qiladi. U BMTning Barqaror Rivojlanish Maqsadlari va Yevropa Ittifoqining ta'limda ekologik barqarorlik bo'yicha majburiyatlariga muvofiq amalga oshirilmoqda.   • 48 ta professor-o'qituvchi va 24 ta ma'muriy xodim maxsus dasturlar orqali o'qitilgan

  • Har bir O'zbekiston hamkorida bittadan 6 ta pilot loyiha

Name of the project:
Green Campus Initiative: Integrating Sustainability into Higher Education in Uzbekistan
Logo of the project:
Acronym:
GreenCamUz
Aim of the project:
The project aims to integrate sustainability into various educational curricula using future-oriented strategies for competence development by project partners. It aligns with the United Nations Sustainable Development Goals and the European Union's commitment to environmental sustainability in education. The initiative will equip students and learners with skills to tackle climate change and ecological challenges while promoting awareness of the links between environmental issues and human activities, preparing them to be responsible global citizens. This effort also supports Uzbekistan's national sustainable development goals.
Main project outcomes:
  • Green campus strategies for 6 universities in Uzbekistan
  • 12 new sustainability-focused courses across various disciplines
  • 3 specialized training courses by EU partners for faculty capacity building
  • 48 faculty members and 24 administrative staff trained through specialized programs
  • Network of 120 trained Green Campus Ambassadors (students and staff)
  • 6 pilot projects, one at each UZ partner, demonstrating sustainable practices
  • Annual Green Campus Conference with 200+ participants
  • Virtual platform for knowledge sharing engaging 1,000+ users
  • Partnerships with 12+ local businesses and community organizations
