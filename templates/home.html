About Green Campus Initiative

A Mission That Unites Generations

Green Campus Initiative: Integrating Sustainability into Higher Education in Uzbekistan (GreenCamUz) is an international project developed by partner higher education institutions from Uzbekistan, Europe, and Türkiye.

The project is funded by a €359,000 European Union grant under the Erasmus+ Capacity Building in Higher Education (CBHE) Strand 1 framework.
