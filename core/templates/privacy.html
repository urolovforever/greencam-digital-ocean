3. What Data We Collect

We may collect the following personal data through this website: name, email address, phone number, and institutional affiliation — only when voluntarily submitted through the contact form. We also collect anonymous usage data through standard web server logs (IP address, browser type, pages visited, time of visit).

4. Purpose of Data Processing

Personal data is processed for the following purposes: responding to inquiries submitted through the contact form, communicating project-related information to stakeholders and partners, and fulfilling reporting obligations to the European Education and Culture Executive Agency (EACEA).

5. Legal Basis

Personal data is processed in compliance with Regulation (EU) 2016/679 (General Data Protection Regulation — GDPR) and applicable national legislation of the Republic of Uzbekistan. Data processing is based on the consent of the data subject (Article 6(1)(a) GDPR) and the legitimate interests of the project consortium (Article 6(1)(f) GDPR).

6. Data Sharing

Personal data may be shared with: project consortium partner institutions (9 beneficiaries listed on the About page), the European Education and Culture Executive Agency (EACEA) for project monitoring and reporting purposes, and auditors or evaluators appointed by the European Commission. We do not sell, trade, or transfer personal data to any third parties for commercial purposes.

7. Data Retention

Personal data will be retained for the duration of the project (36 months) and for a period of 5 years after the final payment, in accordance with Erasmus+ grant agreement requirements (Article 25 of Grant Agreement GAP-101234907).

8. Data Security

We implement appropriate technical and organizational measures to ensure the security of personal data, as required by Article 15.2 of the Grant Agreement.

9. Your Rights

Under the GDPR, you have the right to: access your personal data, rectify inaccurate data, request erasure of your data, restrict processing, object to processing, and data portability. To exercise these rights, contact us at greencamuz@gmail.com.

10. Cookies

This website may use essential cookies for basic functionality. No tracking or advertising cookies are used.

11. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Any changes will be posted on this page with an updated revision date.
