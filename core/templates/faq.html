GreenCamUz loyihasi nima?
GreenCamUz — "Yashil kampus tashabbusi: O'zbekistonda oliy ta'limga barqarorlikni integratsiya qilish" loyihasi bo'lib, Yevropa Ittifoqi tomonidan Erasmus+ CBHE Strand 1 dasturi doirasida moliyalashtirilmoqda. Loyiha O'zbekistondagi 6 ta universitetda barqarorlik tamoyillarini ta'lim jarayoniga integratsiya qilishga qaratilgan.
Loyiha qachon boshlangan va qancha davom etadi?
Loyiha 36 oy davomida amalga oshiriladi (2025–2028).
Loyiha byudjeti qancha?
Yevropa Ittifoqidan €359,682.44 grant ajratilgan.
Loyihada qaysi universitetlar ishtirok etmoqda?
9 ta hamkor universitet: Qo'qon Universiteti (koordinator), Politechnika Opolska (Polsha), Vilnius Tech (Litva), Karabük Universiteti (Türkiye), Toshkent Xalqaro Universiteti (TIU), Markaziy Osiyo Universiteti (CAGU), Alfraganus Universiteti (AFU), Andijon Davlat Texnika Instituti (ASTI), Biznes va Tadbirkorlik Oliy Maktabi (GSBE). Shuningdek 3 ta assotsiatsiyalashgan hamkor: Ekologiya vazirligi, Oliy ta'lim vazirligi va Toshkent Davlat Sharqshunoslik Universiteti.
Loyihaning asosiy maqsadlari nima?
6 ta universitet uchun yashil kampus strategiyalarini ishlab chiqish, 12 ta barqarorlikka yo'naltirilgan yangi kurslar yaratish, 48 ta o'qituvchi va 24 ta ma'muriy xodim malakasini oshirish, 120 ta yashil kampus elchisini tayyorlash, 6 ta pilot loyiha amalga oshirish, yillik Green Campus konferensiya tashkil etish va 1000+ foydalanuvchili virtual platforma yaratish.
Loyiha koordinatori kim?
Qo'qon Universiteti (KU), koordinator — Ulugbek Mamadaliev.
Pilot loyihalar qaysi universitetlarda amalga oshiriladi?
Har bir O'zbekiston hamkorida bittadan: KU — Green Campus Eco-Park, TIU — Smart Waste Management System, CAGU — Solar-Powered Campus Lighting, AFU — Campus Water Conservation System, ASTI — Green Trees Database, GSBE — Air Quality Monitoring.
Loyiha natijalari qayerda e'lon qilinadi?
Barcha ommaviy natijalar greencam.uz saytining "Natijalar" bo'limida va Erasmus+ Project Results platformasida e'lon qilinadi.
Loyihaga qanday qo'shilish mumkin?

What is the GreenCamUz project?
GreenCamUz — "Green Campus Initiative: Integrating Sustainability into Higher Education in Uzbekistan" — is a project funded by the European Union under the Erasmus+ CBHE Strand 1 programme. The project aims to integrate sustainability principles into education at 6 universities in Uzbekistan.
When did the project start and how long does it last?
The project is implemented over 36 months (2025–2028).
What is the project budget?
The EU grant amounts to €359,682.44.
Which universities participate in the project?
9 partner universities: Kokand University (coordinator), Opole University of Technology (Poland), Vilnius Tech (Lithuania), Karabük University (Türkiye), Tashkent International University (TIU), Central Asian University (CAGU), Alfraganus University (AFU), Andijan State Technical Institute (ASTI), Graduate School of Business and Entrepreneurship (GSBE). Plus 3 associated partners: Ministry of Ecology, Ministry of Higher Education, and Tashkent State University of Oriental Studies.
What are the main objectives of the project?
Developing green campus strategies for 6 universities, creating 12 new sustainability-focused courses, training 48 faculty members and 24 administrative staff, training 120 Green Campus Ambassadors, implementing 6 pilot projects, organizing annual Green Campus Conference, and creating a virtual platform for 1,000+ users.
Who is the project coordinator?
Kokand University (KU), coordinator — Ulugbek Mamadaliev.
At which universities will pilot projects be implemented?
One at each UZ partner: KU — Green Campus Eco-Park, TIU — Smart Waste Management System, CAGU — Solar-Powered Campus Lighting, AFU — Campus Water Conservation System, ASTI — Green Trees Database, GSBE — Air Quality Monitoring.
Where are project results published?
All public results are published in the "Results" section of greencam.uz and on the Erasmus+ Project Results platform.
How can I get involved with the project?
For partnerships or questions, please contact us via the "Contact" page: greencamuz@gmail.com
In which languages is the website available?
The website is available in two languages: Uzbek and English.
