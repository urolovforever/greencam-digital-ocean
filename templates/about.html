About GreenCamUz

About the Project

GreenCamUz — Green Campus Initiative: Integrating Sustainability into Higher Education in Uzbekistan — is an international project bringing together higher education institutions from Uzbekistan, Europe, and Türkiye to embed sustainability at the core of academic life.

The project is funded by a €359,000 European Union grant under the Erasmus+ Capacity Building in Higher Education (CBHE) Strand 1 framework, making GreenCamUz the only project of its kind in Uzbekistan under this category. It is implemented in alignment with the United Nations Sustainable Development Goals (SDGs) and the priorities of the European Green Deal.

Our Mission

What We Do

GreenCamUz focuses on three interconnected pillars: modernizing curricula to reflect sustainability principles, building the capacity of academic and administrative staff through targeted green technology training, and launching hands-on pilot initiatives across partner campuses.

Over the course of the project (2025–2028), key outcomes include updated sustainability-oriented curricula across 12 academic disciplines, green technology training for 48 academic and 24 administrative staff members, a Green Campus Ambassadors network of 120 participants, six campus-level pilot projects, an Annual Green Campus Conference with over 200 participants, a virtual knowledge platform serving more than 1,000 users, and active cooperation with over 12 local businesses and non-governmental organizations.

Consortium

Our Partners

The project unites nine higher education institutions across three regions:

  • 🇺🇿 Uzbekistan: Kokand University, Kokand University Andijan Branch, Tashkent International University, Republican Higher School of Business and Entrepreneurship, Central Asian Green University, Andijan State Technical Institute
  • 🇪🇺 Europe: University of Opole (Poland), Vilnius Tech University (Lithuania)
  • 🇹🇷 Türkiye: Karabuk University
Looking Ahead

Our Vision

GreenCamUz is more than a project — it is a long-term commitment to transforming how universities in Uzbekistan think, teach, and operate. By connecting local institutions with European and Turkish partners, we aim to build a generation of graduates and professionals equipped to lead sustainable development in Central Asia and beyond.
